Home

1) Home  Page - A welcoming page that introduces visitors to the site.
2) About Us Page - Information about shop and its mission.
3) Services Page - Details about services provided in shop.
4) Menu Page - A showcase of popular coffee menu and offerings.
5) Blog Page - Showcasing various articles and updates for customers.
6) Contact Page - Provide a way for visitors to get in touch through phone number, email.
7) Booking Page - Where you can reserve your seat when you visit shop.
